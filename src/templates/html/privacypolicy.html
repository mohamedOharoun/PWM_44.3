1. Introduction

Welcome to ©JoinUp! Enterprise. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use our platform to create, join, or manage events, connect with friends, and access additional services, including payment features for premium plans.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: When you register an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional), profile picture, and other identifying information you choose to provide.
  • Event Information: When you create or join events, we collect event details such as event name, date, location, description, and participation status.
  • Friend and Interaction Data: We collect information about your friend connections, favorite events, and any messages exchanged within the platform.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to a premium plan or participate in events requiring payment, we collect payment details, such as credit card information, through a secure third-party payment processor.
  • Usage and Device Information: We collect information about how you use our platform, including log data, IP addresses, browser type, and device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To enable event creation, participation, and social interaction.
  • To process payments for premium features and event fees.
  • To personalize your user experience, including event recommendations and friend suggestions.
  • To communicate updates, notifications, and promotional offers.
  • To improve platform functionality, security, and user satisfaction.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Event Organizers: If you join an event, the organizer can see your profile and participation status.
  • Friends: Your connected friends can see your profile and favorite events.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party providers for payment processing, cloud storage, and platform analytics.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

5. Data Retention and Security

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
